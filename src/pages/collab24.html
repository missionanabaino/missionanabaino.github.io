MA 24 Collaboration Event Recap

The MA 24 Collaboration Event brought together thought leaders and practitioners in the field of church planting to discuss and explore innovative strategies for ministry in the post-Christendom context. Here's a recap of the key moments and discussions.

Highlights

  • Emotional Health Spirituality: Dr. Pablo Polischuk and Tolivar Wills led a deep dive into the importance of emotional health for sustainable ministry.
  • Church Planting Strategies: Interactive sessions on rethinking church organization and evangelistic strategies were facilitated by leading experts.
  • Worship and Reflection: Worship sessions led by Danny Jang and Kevin Nelson provided spiritual nourishment and reflection opportunities.
