Mission Anabaino 2.0

The gist of the 2.0 plan is to continue the MA function as both a Church Planting Network and a Theological collaborative concerning the “Missional Church In The Post Christian Context ”or “Missional Ecclesiology”.

To join the MA team:

If you would like to become a prayer partner only for now with MA—please just email preston@anabaino.org and you will be added to our Quarterly Report.

If you would like to become a prayer partner and invest financially in MA, you may do this in one of three ways:

  • If you are a member of a church that is sponsoring a designated gift to MA, you can give this directly to your church as a collection offering with the Memo to apply to MA
  • Donate Directly To Mission Anabaino via MA website located (upper right hand corner of anabaino.org)
  • Donate directly through https://secure.acceptiva.com/?cst=364ba0
