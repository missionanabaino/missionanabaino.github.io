Our 2024 Plan

Join Our Mission

As we approach 2024, our goal is to significantly impact post-Christendom communities by planting churches dedicated to spreading the Total Christ message. We've been blessed with a strong start, having raised $120,000 of our $360,000 goal, thanks to the generous support of our community and a matching grant that doubles our impact once we reach the halfway mark.

Our mission extends beyond financial contributions; we're building a devoted prayer team to intercede for the success of these church plants. Currently, 504 committed individuals are lifting up this cause, and we invite you to join this vital part of our journey.

Your support, whether through prayer, financial contributions, or both, is crucial. Every donation brings us closer to our goal, and every prayer strengthens our resolve. Together, we can plant seeds of faith in fertile ground, nurturing communities centered around the Total Christ vision.

How You Can Get Involved

  • Donate: Every contribution, large or small, helps us reach our financial goal. Remember, we're just $40,000 away from unlocking an additional $180,000 through our matching grant.
  • Pray: Join our prayer team and commit to regular prayer for the success of our church plants and the communities they will serve.
  • Spread the Word: Share our mission with friends and family who might be interested in supporting our cause.
