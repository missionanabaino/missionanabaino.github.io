Total Christ Vision

The Total Christ Vision is at the core of Mission Anabaino's approach to church planting and missional ecclesiology. This vision is based on a comprehensive understanding of Christ's work and presence in the church and the world.

The Five Marks of the Total Christ Vision

1. Gospel-Centered

We focus on the transformative power of the gospel in all aspects of life and ministry.

2. Sacramental

We emphasize the importance of the sacraments as visible signs of God's grace and presence.

3. Covenantal

We understand the church as a covenant community, bound together by God's promises.

4. Missional

We are committed to engaging with and transforming culture through active mission.

5. Ecclesial

We recognize the church as the primary context for spiritual growth and community.

Implications for Church Planting

The Total Christ Vision shapes our approach to church planting in several ways:

  • Holistic ministry that addresses spiritual, social, and cultural needs
  • Strong emphasis on community formation and discipleship
  • Intentional engagement with local culture and context
  • Focus on developing leaders who embody the Total Christ Vision
