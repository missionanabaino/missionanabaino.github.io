What is Mission Anabaino?

The MA community is comprised of the Staff, MA Board, Anchor churches and their pastors, church planters, interns, prayer and financial supporters, as well as others by invitation from outside entities who are collaborating partners committed to a Total Christ vision in church planting both locally and globally.

Church Planting

Mission Anabaino is dedicated to planting churches where Christ's saving presence is not readily accessible, especially targeting post-Christendom places.

Anchor Churches

Our network is supported by anchor churches that share our theological vision and missional call to plant churches suitable for post-Christian and spiritually frontier regions of the world.

Our Total Christ Vision

Mission Anabaino is a network of churches and individuals who are dedicated to planting churches, supporting the growth of the church planting movement, and connecting with other like-minded people.

We believe that during His ascension ministry today, Christ has an address at every place where there is an apostolic, Christ-centered church.

Our Journey

MA was initially blessed by God through Christ Presbyterian Church, New Haven with $3.5 million venture capital with a vision to plant 10 churches in Southern CT, 10 Churches Globally in TEN YEARS!

Going on nine years, MA has to date been enabled to plant 24 churches and is planning for at least two more in Southern Connecticut. At the same time, more and more church planters from around the country, and even the world, are contacting us, seeking to become theological collaborators in missional ecclesiology and church planters with Mission Anabaino.

MA 2.0

In light of these developments, we are launching MA 2.0. MA 2.0 will become a 501(c)(3) organization governed and supported by a network of member churches and supported by anchor churches. We are currently seeking to recruit potential Member and Anchor Churches who share the MA theological vision and missional call to plant churches suitable to post-Christian and spiritually frontier regions of the world - MA's specialty.
