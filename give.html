Classes

Mission Anabaino is mission “I am ascending” (Greek anabaino, cf. Jn. 20:17). As both a church planting network and theological collaborative, we exist in pursuit of the “greater things” that Christ envisioned for his ascension ministry wherein Christ's saving presence is mediated through the church in every place at the same time where a Total Christ church exists.
